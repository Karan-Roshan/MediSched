FAQ

How do I book an appointment?

Simply download our app or visit the “Book Appointment” section, choose your preferred doctor, and select an available time slot.

Are all doctors verified?

Yes, all our healthcare providers are licensed, background-checked, and verified by medical boards.

Can I get prescriptions online?

Absolutely. After your consultation, your doctor can issue digital prescriptions directly within the app.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or reschedule up to 2 hours before your appointment at no cost.

Can I access past consultations?

Yes. You can view your entire consultation history and reports anytime from your profile dashboard.

Can I choose a specific doctor or specialist?

Yes, you can browse our list of doctors by specialty, experience, and availability, and choose the one that fits your needs.

What should I do if I face technical issues while booking?

You can contact our support team via the Help/Support section in the app or website, and they will assist you promptly.
